maturity
The Maturity Date shall be 30 months DATE from the date of disbursement of Loan Amount.

On the Maturity Date, the Lender shall decide whether they want the Loan Amount along with Interest (“Maturity Amount”) to be repaid or whether they would want the Maturity Amount or part of Maturity Amount to be converted into equity shares of the Borrower ORG . In case of conversion into equity shares, the conversion shall happen at the valuation of Borrower PERSON to be Rs.5,00,00,000/- (Rupees Five Crores).

In case the Lender does not convert the entire or any of the Maturity Amount into equity, the repayment shall happen in instalments to be mutually decided by the Parties.

Time is of essence in the Agreement LAW and the Maturity Date is non-extendible. However, at the written request of the Borrower ORG , the Lender may, in its sole discretion, extend the Maturity Date on such terms and conditions as it may deem fit.
